Shubhendu Anand

Partner, Atharva Legal LLP

CMr. Shubhendu Anand is an advocate, practicing primarily in the Supreme Court of India and is a Partner at Atharva Legal LLP. He is Managing Trustee of Rta Foundation, a conservative think tank in India. He obtained his post-graduate degree in Intellectual Property Law from Queen Mary, University of London. Shubhendu regularly writes legal columns for leading magazines & newspapers and his research articles have been published by leading legal and medical journals of the country.

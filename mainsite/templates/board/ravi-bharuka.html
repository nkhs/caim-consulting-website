Ravi Bharuka

Advocate on Record, Supreme Court of India

Mr. Ravi Bharuka is a Partner at GDG LAW LLP and a 2013 law graduate from ILS Law College, Pune. He has worked with GDG since then and has successfully worked on various commercial disputes including arbitration involving heavy financial stakes and complex questions of facts and law before the Arbitral Tribunal (both Institutional and ad hoc), High Court and Supreme Court. He has been declared as an Advocate-on-Record by the Supreme Court of India in May 2020. He is also an Advocate-on-Record at the Patna High Court since August, 2017. Ravi has also completed membership course of Chartered Institute of Arbitrator in March 2017.

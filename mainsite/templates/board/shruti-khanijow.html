Shruti Khanijow

Principal Associate, Khaitan & Co.

Currently working as a principal associate in the commercial disputes practice of Khaitan & Co., Shruti has represented private parties and State entities in institutional (ICC, LCIA and SIAC) and ad hoc arbitrations seated in India and abroad. She is experienced in advising clients on construction and energy arbitrations, shareholder disputes, product liability litigation, corporate criminal liability and disparagement and defamation claims. She holds an LL.M. in International Law from University of Cambridge and was a member of the editorial board of Cambridge International Law Journal and Cambridge Law Review.

About Us

‘Caim Consulting’ is a non-profit initiative pioneered with an aim to deliver cost free legal assistance to start-ups, innovations and businesses, especially those in their incubatory phases.

Like we state “we assist, you thrive”

Dedicated Team

Caim features a highly motivated team of individuals who consistently work towards the enhancement and upliftment of promising businesses by aiding them with the catena of legal and financial services incumbent for establishment and growth.

Assured Quality Service

Besides, the quality of services we provide is underpinned by some of the excellent professionals who have shown their amenability to constitute the Advisory Board to serve our customers better.

Reliable Platform

Caim is a unique support platform to catalyse the fluent realisation of your business ideas and simultaneously ensuring that your prospects are not hindered by any financial or referential constraints.

Our Vision

At Caim, we harbour a belief that creativity and innovation shall never be denied support. We could not possibly think of a world without dynamism which essentially comes from the pillars of entrepreneurship i.e. creativity and innovation. Keeping our vision in consonance with our motto, we endeavour to reach out to those young businesses that have covered an extra mile to take their ideations into the phase of implementation.

We are sincerely committed to provide them with all the support within our reach and power to keep them standing and competing at par with other established businesses in the industry. Caim intends to hold their hands and assist them in crossing the choppy waters of incubation and make valuable additions to their journey.

As far as the interests of Caim are concerned, we are fortunate to have found our way of returning the favour to the business industry that has made our country one of the largest economies in the world. Thus we are happily waiving off all kinds of charges for our services and going absolutely cost free for India.

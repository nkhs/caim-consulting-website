Services

Our esteemed services include the following domains.
Besides Caim Consultancy provides other legal and financial services relating to your business to help your business grow.

Business Incorporation

We understand your business objective and will help you to opt for the most appropriate legal structure to run your business.

Intellectual Property Protection

We will shield your innovation and ideas from being duplicated or utilized in any case other than a legitimate consent

Business Contracts

We recognise the agreements your business executes with third parties and different stakeholders which we will be drafting to serve your needs

Legal Compliances

Ministry of Corporate Affairs, India poses loads of mandatory compliances which we will keep a track of to stimulate effective functioning.

Quality Certification & Licensing

Your business and business products are subject to compulsory licenses and ISO certification, for hassle free operation we will carry the necessary filings.

Due Diligence

Our team will be vigilant with regards to statutory records and all other principal and ancillary corporate documentation coupled with advisory and course of action in case of any default.

Reliable
